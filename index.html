📋 HOW TO PLAY

🎯 Objective
Name an athlete who played for both teams on your square. Players alternate turns on a 3×3 grid.
⚡ Steal
Guess into an opponent's square. If your player has a lower rarity %, you take the square.
🏆 Winning — Hold the Line
Get 3-in-a-row and hold it for 3 consecutive turns. Your opponent must break it before time runs out.
💥 Sudden Death
If both players hold 3-in-a-row simultaneously, a 5-turn countdown begins. Break their line or the lower average rarity wins.
📋 Miss Streak
5 consecutive misses ends the game. Most squares wins; ties go to lower total rarity.
🔍 Hints
3 hints per player. Reveals jersey number, position, and draft year of the most common valid answer that can improve a square.
📊 Rarity %
Lower % = rarer player. Harder to steal, better in tiebreaks.
⏱ Timer
1:30 per turn. Running out auto-passes your turn.
